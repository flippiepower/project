Insights from this project

In order for humans and wild animals to coexist in urban areas constant trade-offs need to be made. Different stakeholders and disciplines are involved in these trade-offs such as: urban developers, ecologists, government etc. As wild life can't speak for itself it is the task of humans to make sure there is a safe place for them.

Innovative solutions can help with living in coexistance, such as bee hotels. These innovations assist wild life to adapt to an urbanized environment.
